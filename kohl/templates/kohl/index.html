How to buy KOHL Coin?

KOHL can be bought on QuickSwap. We explain how to connect to the Polygon Network.

It's simple: go to https://polygonscan.com/

Scroll to the bottom of the page, click Add Polygon Network to connect your Metamask to the Mainnet RPC.

Bridge your MATIC from Ethereum Network to Polygon Network using the link https://wallet.polygon.technology/

You're almost there! Deposit MATIC from L1 into the bridge by confirming the transaction in your Metamask.

Wait for the transaction to confirm, switch to Polygon Network on your Metamask and your funds will be there when the L2 bridge transaction confirms!.

Some exchanges such as Binance allow users to withdraw MATIC directly to Polygon Mainnet. If you're on Binance, that's the simplest and fastest way. If you're not, try to find out if your exchange does Polygon withdrawals.
